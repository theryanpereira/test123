About

Strategist. Engineer.
Storyteller.

My background is strategy and storytelling. My current obsession is agentic engineering. Most people land on one side of that line: they understand the business deeply, or they know how to build. I have always wanted to do both, and AI finally made that realistic.

I have scaled brands across global markets, shipped digital products that moved real numbers, and run customer success at Sprinklr, IKEA India, and Titan Company in ways that made retention a revenue conversation. The thing that consistently moved people to buy, to stay, to care was a well-told story backed by a sharp strategy.

AI pulled me toward engineering because it is the highest-leverage thing I have found. So I started building with it: Streamlit apps, Groq-powered tools, agentic workflows, generative image pipelines. I build the things, then tell the story of why they matter.

What I am working toward is a team building something real with AI, where the engineering, the strategy, and the narrative are part of the same job.
